Arknights Damage Calculator

How to use ?

Attack : Enter the operator's maximum attack value after applying skills and buffs.

Hits : The number of damage instances per attack.

Attack Interval : The time between each attack in seconds. This is used for calculating DPS and can be left empty.

Flat Debuff & Ignore : Reduce enemy stats by a fixed amount. For example, Ifrit's S3 applies a 20 RES debuff, and Surtr's talent ignores 20 RES.

percentage Debuff & Ignore : Reduce enemy stats by a percentage. For example, Schwarz's talent applies a 25% DEF debuff, and Nearl the Radiant Knight's talent ignores 28% DEF.

Damage Multiplier : Multiplies the operator's current attack when dealing damage. For example, Pallas's S1 deals 175% of her attack, meaning it has a 1.75 multiplier, and her trait deals 120%, adding another 1.2 multiplier.

Percentage Damage Amplification : Increases the damage an enemy takes by a percentage. For example, effects like Fragile or Saria's S3 apply a 55% Arts damage amplifier.
